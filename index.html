🕉️ About Kumbh Mela

The Nashik Kumbh Mela is one of the world's largest spiritual events, held every 12 years at the sacred Trimbakeshwar and the Godavari River. Millions gather to bathe in the holy river, seeking spiritual cleansing and blessings.

🛕 Sacred Rituals

  • Shahi Snan (Royal Bath)
  • Peshwai Processions
  • Spiritual Discourses & Pravachans
  • Sadhu Gatherings and Darshan

🗺️ Nearby Attractions

  • Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga
  • Panchavati & Sita Gufa
  • Muktidham Temple
  • Anjneri Hills (Birthplace of Hanuman)
