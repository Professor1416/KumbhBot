🌿 Eco-Friendly Travel Tips

Preserve the sanctity of Kumbh Mela and Mother Earth

♻️ Sustainable Travel

  • Use public transport like buses or shared cabs
  • Walk or use bicycles for short distances
  • Book accommodations close to the mela zone

🚯 Reduce Waste

  • Carry reusable bottles, utensils, and bags
  • Avoid single-use plastics at food stalls or prasad counters
  • Dispose of waste only in segregated bins

🌱 Support Green Initiatives

  • Attend environment awareness sessions at the venue
  • Buy from eco-conscious vendors and NGOs
  • Plant a sapling through verified local drives

🙏 Pilgrim’s Oath

“I commit to protecting the sacred Godavari and leaving no trace behind.”
