✅ Do’s & 🚫 Don’ts

Navigate Kumbh Mela respectfully and safely

✅ Do’s

  • Carry a copy of your ID and emergency contact
  • Respect the traditions, saints, and fellow devotees
  • Use designated bathing zones only
  • Wear comfortable, modest clothes suited for walking
  • Follow instructions from police and volunteers
  • Use bio-toilets and keep the ghats clean
  • Donate food or essentials at official donation centers

🚫 Don’ts

  • Don’t push or rush into crowded areas during holy dips
  • Don’t photograph sadhus or pilgrims without consent
  • Don’t litter or pollute the river or ghats
  • Don’t carry non-vegetarian food, alcohol, or tobacco
  • Don’t spread rumors or engage in arguments
  • Don’t feed monkeys or stray animals

🕊️ Be a Responsible Pilgrim

Your actions contribute to the spiritual and cultural sanctity of Kumbh Mela. Follow the guidelines to make it a peaceful experience for all.
