🍛 Food & Prasad Guide

Taste the spirit of Kumbh – safely and traditionally

🍽️ Local Vegetarian Cuisine

  • Maharashtrian Thali – Bhakri, Pithla, Rice, Pickle
  • Street snacks – Misal Pav, Kachori, Samosa, Poha
  • Seasonal fruits, sugarcane juice, and lassi

Prefer hygienic eateries near Panchavati and Trimbak Road.

🙏 Prasad Offerings

  • Available at major temples like Trimbakeshwar & Muktidham
  • Common prasad: Ladoo, Halwa, Dry fruits
  • Ask for packed options to carry home safely

💧 Hygiene & Safety Tips

  • Carry your own bottled water or use trusted kiosks
  • Avoid raw foods or uncovered sweets from roadside stalls
  • Wash hands regularly or use sanitizer

🛍️ Where to Eat

  • Annakshetras (Free Community Meals)
  • ISKCON Tent Kitchens
  • Clean restaurants: Hotel Panchavati, Rasoi Pure Veg, Barbeque Ville
