🛕 How to Reach Nashik Kumbh Mela 2027

Plan your journey with ease – by air, rail, or road

✈️ By Air

The nearest airport is Ozar Airport (ISK), about 20 km from Nashik city. Major airports nearby:

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai (BOM) – 170 km
  • Pune Airport (PNQ) – 210 km

You can hire taxis or use buses to reach Nashik from these airports.

🚆 By Train

Nashik Road Railway Station (station code: NK) is well connected:

  • From Mumbai (3.5 hours)
  • From Pune, Delhi, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, etc.
  • Check availability: ConfirmTKT

🚌 By Bus

MSRTC, RedBus, and private operators provide buses from major cities.

  • Book via RedBus
  • Frequent buses from Mumbai, Pune, Aurangabad, and Shirdi

🚘 By Car

You can drive via NH160 or NH60. Ample parking facilities will be arranged during Kumbh. Use Google Maps or Ola/Uber apps for directions and bookings.

📍 Navigation Tips

  • Use Google Maps to locate key sites like Trimbakeshwar and Tapovan
  • Follow signboards and police instructions during mela days
  • Keep offline maps for emergency navigation
